Solar energy.

Energy that comes directly from the sun: photovoltaic cells (usually consisting of a silicon substrate, arranged in large arrays) convert the sun's rays directly into electrical energy. In some cases, the heat produced by sunlight is also used to produce electricity, this is known as solar thermal energy.
